Privacy Policy for Debtless

Effective Date: December 19, 2024

1. Information Collection

Debtless does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or data from users. All data you enter (debts, savings goals, etc.) is stored locally on your device and is not shared with anyone, including the developer.

2. In-App Purchases

Debtless uses Apple's in-app purchase system for optional "Tip the Developer" features. All payment processing is handled securely by Apple. The app does not access or store any payment information.

3. Analytics & Tracking

Debtless does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

4. Data Security

Your data never leaves your device unless you back it up using iCloud or your own device backup. The app does not transmit any data to external servers.

5. Children's Privacy

Debtless is not intended for children under the age of 13. No personal information is knowingly collected from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated policy will be made available in the app and on this page.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact: your@email.com
